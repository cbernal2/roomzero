'Nothing Happens' (2019) by Wallows





This album was a major personal soundtrack during the slow, uninspiring, state of limbo that defined the COVID-19 era. My cousin had gifted me this record for my 17th birthday. I remember being extremely excited, I listened to the record front to back several times over with my eyes closed, laying on my bed.
