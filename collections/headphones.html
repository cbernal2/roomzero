Srythm Headphones





These headphones were one of the first purchases I made before leaving for my first year of university.


I have no idea why I decided that this would be my gift to myself before moving away—especially since I typically hate wearing things on my head. But they ended up being one of the most useful, and easily my favourite, electronics I own.


Now, having lived with them for three years, I’d recommend a pair to any student. They’ve allowed me to escape into my own world while in crowded, overstimulating environments—an essential for any university student in a shared dorm or on the fourth floor of the McLaughlin Library.
